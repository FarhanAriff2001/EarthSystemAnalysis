Developed a series of data analysis and signal processing projects focused on Earth and space science phenomena using Python. Examples such as analyzing large time-series datasets related to El Niño–Southern Oscillation (ENSO), atmospheric CO₂ concentrations, and solar activity. Implemented data preprocessing, visualization, and spectral analysis techniques including Fast Fourier Transform (FFT), harmonic analysis, and short-time Fourier analysis to identify periodic patterns and anomalies. Interpreted results to understand climate variability, atmospheric trends, and solar cycles through quantitative and visual analysis.
